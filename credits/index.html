Divide and Conquer

Divide and Conquer is an overhaul of the Third Age Total War mod (“TATW”). TATW is integrated into the Divide & Conquer mod.

We give our whole hearted thanks to the TATW team and all they have done for the modding community of Medieval II TW.

Without you, none of this would have been possible.
